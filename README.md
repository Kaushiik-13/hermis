# Hermis

AI-powered content pipeline that turns technical sources into platform-ready drafts — in your voice, zero AI slop. Built for DevRel professionals and technical content teams.

Hermis helps technical creators move from source material to publish-ready drafts without losing the human voice that makes technical content worth reading.

It is built for the work that usually sits around writing: tracking product updates, reading research, filtering repeated stories, choosing what matters, and reshaping ideas for different platforms.

The goal is simple: keep the human in control, remove the pipeline grunt work, and make every draft feel like it came from the person publishing it.

## Product Principles

- Zero AI slop. Generic phrasing, filler transitions, and template-like writing should not reach the approval queue.
- Sounds like the user. Voice is the primary constraint; platform formatting comes after.
- Human approval first. Hermis prepares drafts, but nothing posts without review.
